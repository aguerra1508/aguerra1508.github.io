About Me

Hi I'm Analee Guerra. I currently live in San Antonio, TX with my husband and three dogs: Dexter, Bailey and Gomez. I am a proud member of the Fightin' Texas Aggie Class of 2012 (Whoop!). I have degree's in Microbiology and Clinical Laboratory Sciences. Currently I work as a Medical Laboratory Scientist at University Hospital. Also, I am currently enrolled in the UTSA Coding Bootcamp program.

In my spare time, I love going hunting, fishing and being in the outdoors with my husband. Health and fitness are also passions of mine. I am a certifed CrossFit Level 1 and USA Weightlifting Level 1 Coach. I have been doing CrossFit and Bikram yoga for the past 7 years.
