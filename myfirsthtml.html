How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Get two slices of bread, peanut butter and jelly
  2. Using a knife, slather one slice of bread with the peanut butter
  3. Then slather the other slice of bread with jelly
  4. Put the two slices of bread together and enjoy
